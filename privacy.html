隱私政策 / Privacy Policy

最後更新 Last updated: 2026-07-10

「Sheila 社群自動發文」(以下稱「本應用程式」)是一個內部使用的社群內容管理工具,用於協助授權帳號的內容排程、發佈與成效分析。本應用程式僅供帳號擁有者本人及其授權的內容管理者使用,不對公眾開放註冊。

"Sheila Social Publisher" (the "App") is an internal social-media content management tool that helps authorized accounts schedule and publish content and analyze performance. It is used solely by the account owners and their authorized content manager, and is not open for public registration.

我們收集的資料 / Data We Collect

本應用程式透過 Meta 官方 API(Threads API、Instagram Graph API、Facebook Pages API)在取得帳號擁有者明確授權後,存取以下資料:帳號基本資料(用戶名稱、帳號編號)、貼文內容與公開成效數據(觀看、按讚、留言、分享數),以及以關鍵字搜尋取得的公開貼文內容(用於內容趨勢分析)。

With the explicit authorization of the account owners, the App accesses via official Meta APIs: basic profile data (username, user ID), the accounts' own posts and public performance metrics (views, likes, replies, shares), and public posts retrieved via keyword search (for content-trend analysis).

資料的使用方式 / How Data Is Used

資料僅用於:(1) 為授權帳號排程與發佈內容;(2) 分析授權帳號自身貼文成效;(3) 彙整公開內容趨勢作為內容企劃參考。資料不會出售、出租或提供給任何第三方,也不用於廣告投放或用戶追蹤。

Data is used only to (1) schedule and publish content for the authorized accounts, (2) analyze the accounts' own post performance, and (3) summarize public content trends for editorial planning. Data is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties, and is not used for advertising or user tracking.

資料儲存與安全 / Storage & Security

存取權杖與分析資料儲存於帳號管理者的私人裝置,以作業系統層級的檔案權限保護,不存放於公開伺服器。公開貼文的趨勢彙整僅保留統計摘要,不建立任何第三方用戶的個人檔案。

Access tokens and analytics are stored on the administrator's private devices, protected by OS-level file permissions, and are not hosted on public servers. Trend summaries of public posts keep aggregate statistics only; no profiles of third-party users are created.

資料刪除 / Data Deletion

帳號擁有者可隨時撤銷授權(於 Threads/Instagram/Facebook 的應用程式與網站權限設定中移除本應用程式),撤銷後本應用程式即無法再存取其資料;並可透過下方聯絡方式要求刪除已儲存的資料,我們將於 30 天內完成刪除。詳見資料刪除指示。

Account owners may revoke authorization at any time via their Threads/Instagram/Facebook app permissions, after which the App can no longer access their data. Stored data can be deleted upon request via the contact below within 30 days. See Data Deletion Instructions.
