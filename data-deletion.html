資料刪除指示 / Data Deletion Instructions

最後更新 Last updated: 2026-07-10

如要刪除「Sheila 社群自動發文」所存取或儲存與你帳號相關的資料,請依下列任一方式:

To delete data accessed or stored by "Sheila Social Publisher" in connection with your account, use either method below:

方式一:撤銷授權 / Revoke Authorization

  1. Threads:設定 → 帳號 → 網站權限 → 移除「Sheila 社群自動發文」(Threads: Settings → Account → Website permissions → remove the app)
  2. Instagram / Facebook:設定 → 應用程式和網站 → 移除「Sheila 社群自動發文」(IG/FB: Settings → Apps and Websites → remove the app)

撤銷後,本應用程式立即失去存取權。After revocation the App immediately loses access.

方式二:來信要求刪除 / Request by Email

寄信至 lovesea2433@hotmail.com,註明你的帳號用戶名稱,我們將於 30 天內刪除所有已儲存與你帳號相關的資料,並回信確認。

Email lovesea2433@hotmail.com with your username; all stored data related to your account will be deleted within 30 days and confirmed by reply.
